I love you ❤️

This is just a small way to say how much you mean to me.

In the quiet moments when the world is loud,

Your patience is the anchor, steady and unbowed.

With a heart of gold and a spirit so light,

Your kindness turns the darkest hours bright.

You lead with grace and a gentle hand,

The most beautiful soul in all the land.

In every action, your character shines true,

I’m forever grateful for the gift of you.

And when the day ends and you’re finally deep in sleep,

There’s a cute, quiet magic that you always keep.

Even your dreams have a sweet, funny charm—

The most peaceful sight, tucked right in my arm.

Thank you for being you. ❤️
